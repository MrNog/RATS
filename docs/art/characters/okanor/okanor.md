## Locked look (never changes between images)

  healer: a dark-knight who wields the Light, NOT a soft angelic saint. Battle-worn, grim resolve.

  Dark and grim — **NOT ivory / white / pale**.

  **angular flanged head** (bladed/spiked flanges, ornate gold-and-iron), imposing; **NOT a small round

  per hand — never stacked. NO extra weapons.
- **Signature detail:** the **burning amber rune-glow** running the seams of his dark plate + the hot
  flanged mace — the Light forged into a war-mace, not a soft angelic aura. Render CLEAN (radiant
  edge-light, NOT floating ember particles or speckled dots).
