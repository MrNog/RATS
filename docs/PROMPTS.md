# 🐀 RATS — Image Prompt Library

Reusable ChatGPT/DALL-E image prompts for the guild's hero art (officers, Fangs, lore).
Keep this consistent so every new piece matches the gallery. Local-only notes (gitignored).

> Tip: ChatGPT **recreates** the RATS logo / Horde insignia from the text — it won't copy your
> exact PNG. For a pixel-perfect logo, generate the art first, then paste the `RATS` PNG into the
> bottom-right in an image editor.

---

## 🎨 Locked style tokens (use in every prompt)

- **Format:** cinematic widescreen — `21:9` for character cards, `16:9` for big army scenes, `3:1` for banners.
- **Art style:** "World of Warcraft cinematic concept art, painterly brushwork, highly detailed,
  subtle film-grain texture overlay."
- **Subjects:** anthropomorphic rats — dark fur, prominent whiskers, sharp fierce eyes, clawed hands,
  partially visible tail.
- **Title font:** rough hand-painted **brush-stroke lettering**, thick uneven strokes, visible bristle
  texture + grunge torn edges.
  - Officers/normal cards: **sandy tan-gold `#C8A96E`**.
  - Fangs (grim/elite): **deep blood-red** with the grey tagline below.
- **Horde symbol divider:** small Horde insignia flanked by short lines: `—⧖—`.
- **Guild logo:** BOTTOM-RIGHT, small like a signature watermark — word "RATS" in sandy tan-gold
  brush-stroke lettering immediately followed by the black-and-gold Horde insignia, faint glow,
  never competing with the characters.
- **Mood:** dark, honorable but fierce. Dark/desaturated environment so characters read clearly.
- **CLEAN RENDER (ALWAYS, every prompt):** clear air — NO floating dust motes, NO ember particles, NO
  speckled glowing dots, NO heavy haze. Razor-sharp focus, especially on the rat's face and hands;
  faces big and readable; correct head-to-body proportion; don't oversize the subject. Smooth light,
  no blown-out bloom.
- **Always DO NOT:** floating dust/ember particles, speckled noise, heavy haze, oversized/indistinct
  subject, modern clothing, human faces, Alliance symbols, excessive bloom that hides the characters,
  plain white background.

## 📐 TEMPLATE F — Solo officer card (CLEAN) — 21:9

The signature individual hero card (matches the original `Rellik 1.png`). One rat per card.

**Golden rules (what makes it clean — learned the hard way):**
1. **Centered name** — brush-stroke name across the **TOP CENTER**, directly above the rat (NOT pushed
   to a side). Sandy tan-gold `#C8A96E`, grunge bristle texture. Title below in wide-spaced caps, then
   `—⧖—` Horde divider. Small `RATS` + Horde insignia watermark bottom-right.
2. **Framing** — full standing figure in the **central third**; face big, clear and readable (eyes,
   snout, whiskers, fur), clawed hands visible. **Do NOT oversize the rat** (don't fill the whole
   frame) and don't pull so wide the head reads small. Leave headroom + floor.
3. **Render quality** — CLEAN & crisp. **NO floating dust motes / ember particles / speckled glowing
   dots / heavy haze.** Sharp focus, smooth light. (This was the #1 thing that muddied early tries.)
4. **Equipped gear** — weapons/shields worn or held by the character, not propped separately.
5. **Different pose AND different ambient per officer** — do NOT reuse the ruined-battlefield-with-
   broken-towers-banners-and-stuck-swords scene; that one belongs to Rellik's original. Each officer
   below has its own world.

### Per-officer pose + ambient (locked)

- **GRUNHO — "RAT KING"** (Prot Warrior): wide commanding stance, iron crown, tower shield EQUIPPED on
  left forearm, warblade point-down. **Ambient:** throne-hall of a captured keep — dark pillars,
  shadowed throne, hanging Horde banners, brazier firelight + cool moonlight. Gold/charcoal/blood-red.
- **FOUG — "THE PROPHET"** (Hunter): dynamic 3/4 side, drawing the longbow with a glowing spectral
  arrow, mist-beast companion lunging beside. **Ambient:** windswept Highland ridge at dawn — fog, blue

- **OKANOR — "THE LIGHTKEEPER"** (Holy Paladin): slightly low angle, golden mace raised with a holy

- **KOBE — "MASTER OF SHADOWS"** (Rogue): crouched low on a rooftop ledge / gargoyle perch, twin

  rooftops, broken spires, lanterns, pale moon, rain streaks. Black/crimson/steel-blue + amber.
- **ARDIL — "CLAWFATHER"** (Feral Druid): mid feral lunge / roar, claws crackling emerald, half-
